EASIEST WAY (NO INSTALLATION):
================================

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/new

2. Sign up (FREE - use email or GitHub)

3. Drag your entire project folder OR click "Browse" and select this folder

4. Click "Deploy"

5. DONE! Your site is LIVE! 🎉


YOUR PORTFOLIO WILL BE AT:
https://your-project-name.vercel.app
